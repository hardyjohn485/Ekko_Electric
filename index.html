ABOUT US

About ekko electric

Welcome to ekko electric, a specialized manufacturer of high-quality Low Tension (LT) panel boards. Based in Kottayam, Kerala, we design, manufacture, and supply standard and custom-built panel boards that serve a variety of industries.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and performance has established us as a trusted partner for reliable power distribution and control. As a distinguished name in panel board manufacturing in kerala, we are proud to deliver superior electrical control panels engineered for modern industrial needs.
